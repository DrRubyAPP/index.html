Distribution Strategy · 3-Stage Narrative Arc

Nobody knows DrRuby yet. Use the strongest asset to get attention — then transfer ownership to Ruby.
Stage 1 · 2026 · Borrow

Lead with Brenner

Use Brenner's scientific authority to earn media attention and investor credibility. Headlines can feature his name. Ruby is always the interviewer — never absent from the frame.

Stage 2 · 2027 · Transition

Ruby takes center stage

Shift headline framing to Ruby's story and DrRuby's mission. Brenner becomes one of several expert voices. Women's health narrative moves to the front.

Stage 3 · 2028 · Own

DrRuby is the news

Product growth, user stories, and Ruby's founder brand carry all distribution. Experts reinforce; they no longer lead. Brand equity fully owned by DrRuby.

⚠️ Risk to avoid: "DrRuby = Brenner Fan Club." The structure is always: Ruby asks. Brenner answers. Ruby holds the mic in every format, every platform, every clip.

Guiding Principles

No chasing virality

The 90-day goal is consistent brand identity, not traffic spikes. Stability builds trust. One strong piece beats three mediocre ones.

LinkedIn is the anchor

Ruby already has 2,375 followers there. This is the highest-leverage platform for investor and partner outreach. Always publish here first.

Ruby holds the mic. Experts answer.

Ruby HealthSpan Lab (@rubyhealthspanlab) — Ruby is always the interviewer. Brenner is the first featured scientist, not the anchor of the brand. Narrative ownership stays with Ruby regardless of who's on screen.

Keep the two tracks distinct

Ruby IP = ideas, philosophy, founder journey. DrRuby = product, technology, skin data. Never conflate them in the same post.

Four Content Pillars

Every piece of content maps to one of these. If it doesn't, don't publish it.
Pillar 01

Science vs. Hype

Ruby debunks wellness myths using scientific evidence. Experts (Brenner, Gunter, dermatologists) are sources — Ruby is the narrator. "Ruby asks what science actually knows. Then we tell you." This is DrRuby's core differentiator.

Pillar 02

The Founder's Reality

Four companies, two exits, building with world-class scientists. The authentic, unpolished story of what it actually takes — not the highlight reel.

Pillar 03

Gift of Time

"I don't help you spend more time managing your health. I help you get that time back." Ruby's life philosophy — and DrRuby's product promise.

Pillar 04

The Cross-border Creator

Artist + founder + investor. Chinese-American. Silicon Valley insider. Her multidimensional identity is rare in healthtech — lean into it, don't hide it.

Content Voice

How Ruby sounds across all platforms

Direct

No hedging, no vague wellness language. Say what you mean. "This supplement has no evidence for your goal" — not "results may vary."

Evidence-first

Every claim is defensible. If Brenner wouldn't sign off on it, don't say it. Credibility is the product.

Human

You're an artist, a founder, a person. Not a brand. Share the real moments — the hard decisions, the surprising conversations, the self-doubt.
